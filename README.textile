h1. Hub - A central place for publications

Hub is a simple script to manage the publication of you Jekyll content on various websites.

All happends in the Rakefile, have a look and customize it your way to publish your sites.

h2. Requirements

* Your configured "Github Page":http://you.github.com/ if you to publish
	your content in it
* A "Jekyll":http://github.com/mojombo/jekyll/ clone in $ROOT/jekyll/
* "The Git gem":http://github.com/schacon/ruby-git from Scott
